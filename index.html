About Me

While in High school, I was always fascinated with the criminal justice system. I used to love watching the show 'Criminal Minds'. After High school, I went to college to study criminal justice. When I graduated, I joined a federal agency doing testing. While I was working for this agency, I always had Penelope, the technical analyst that was always helping the agents solve complicated cases with her computer skills, in the back of my mind. While working in testing, I was also working on my software engineering skills. When the pandemic hit, there was less work to do which, allowed me to focus more on software engineering skills that I have been developing for a while. I took on some freelance projects and open sourcing projects, and now, I am deciding to go down this career path instead of going back to testing as things open up.
