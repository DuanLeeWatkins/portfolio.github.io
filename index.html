About Me

After Graduating in 2016, I was able to achieve a position as a Drug Testing Technician at a Criminal Justice federal agency. For several years working as a Drug Tech, I realized that having a career in the criminal justice field was not my true passion. I have always been skilled with computers and technology. I find pleasure in troubleshooting and seeing through projects from start to finish. When the pandemic started, it gave me the extra free time to dive deeper into my interests in tech and software engineering. Not only did I have a passion for tech, but I also have a passion for music and music production. Being able to combine my two passions of engineering and music is a long time goal that I want to accomplish, but I am excited to see where the road will take to get there. I am committed to showing what an asset I can be to the company and the team I join. I am excited to join a company like yours where I can bring my passions to life, serve the company and the community, and work alongside like-minded individuals that are excited to build products and help people.
