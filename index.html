About Duan

After I graduated from college, I became a Drug Testing technician for people who are under parole and probation. I also enjoyed music producing and learning about creating webpages and software.

As a student at Thinkful, I have begun my jounrney to become a software developer and I could not be more excited! If you are looking to hire a new developer please reach out! I am so excited to learn, build, and grow during this new career path.
